About Me

I am an architectural designer balancing advanced academic research with active professional practice. Currently pursuing an MSc in Architecture, I use my postgraduate studies to deepen my understanding of spatial design, sustainable systems, and contemporary architectural theory.

My professional background includes two years of practical industry exposure. Following a dedicated one-year internship, I have spent the past year working part-time within the field alongside my Master's program. This academic-professional balance allows me to continuously test theoretical concepts in real-world environments.

My practice operates on both a local and global scale. Regionally, I contribute to hands-on architectural projects from concept through to detailing. Internationally, I leverage advanced digital tools to provide high-fidelity 3D rendering and visualization services, helping design teams abroad translate their complex concepts into clear, evocative imagery.
